About Us

Nexus Textile is a company that produces fabrics and sportswear in Turkey (Bursa). Our company has been producing textiles, women's sportswear and offering world-class service at reasonable prices for the past 15 years. The company develops its own production and fruitfully cooperates with leading Turkish and foreign wholesale companies selling sportswear, and also actively promotes its products to the markets of the CIS countries and Europe.


Our company also produces corporate clothing, clothing for promotions and advertising events, clothing for employees of the hotel and restaurant business, clothing for gifts to your employees and partners with print and embroidery. The quality of our goods, a wide range and low prices - this is what attracts our customers. We are always open to all kinds of proposals. If you need something unusual, non-standard and original and you don't know where to order it, just contact us and our company will bring your idea to life.
